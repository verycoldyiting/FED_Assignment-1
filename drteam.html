Dr Peh Yew Jia

Dr Peh Yew Jia is a registered Orthodontic Specialist with the Singapore Dental Council since 2017. She graduated in 2008 from the National University of Singapore (NUS) with a Bachelor of Dental Surgery and went on to pursue her passion for Orthodontics with a scholarship from the National Dental Centre Singapore (NDCS). She completed her Master of Dental Surgery (Orthodontics) from NUS in 2014, with dual accreditation from the Royal College of Surgeons Edinburgh.

Prior to joining private practice, she was an Associate Consultant in the department of Orthodontics in NDCS. She is also a visiting clinical tutor to the Postgraduate Orthodontic Programme at NUS. Dr Peh has published her research in a peer-reviewed journal. She is an active member of the Association of Orthodontists (Singapore), and was in the executive committee from 2017-2020.

Preventative Dentistry

Dr Peh has been to Thambuchet and Syabruk Beshe, Nepal, to provide basic dental treatment to the villagers in the secluded mountainous areas.
