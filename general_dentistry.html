General Dentistry

General dentistry refers to dental treatments that can prevent, restore, and enhance your dental and oral health. Dentists are often the primary providers of general dentistry, otherwise known as general dental care. This can be care issued through oral health examinations, professional teeth cleanings, or dental fillings.

General Dentistry Services

At Kho Dental Clinic, we offer general dentistry services that can be divided into the following categories:

Preventative Dentistry

Preventative dentistry is a critical step in preserving optimal oral and dental health. As the saying goes, prevention is better than cure. Following active preventative measures effectively wards off tooth decay and gum disease.

Our dentists do not just provide treatments for your dental ailments. We also provide comprehensive guidance on how to enhance your dental hygiene practices at home.

Part of preventative dentistry includes:

  • Routine dental examinations with X-rays
  • Regular maintenance
  • Professional teeth cleaning
  • Dental fillings
  • Fissure sealants
  • Periodontal treatment
  • Root canal treatment
  • Tooth extractions

Restorative Dentistry

Restorative dentistry helps restore your teeth to their original looks and function. While our dentists at Kho Dental Clinic are committed to maintaining the functionality of your natural teeth as long as possible, in some cases, this is not possible.

However, with the help of modern technology, dental clinics like Kho Dental Clinic are now able to offer a wide range of teeth restoration options that are tailored to your specific needs and preferences. Some of these treatment options include:

  • Dental implants
  • Dental crowns
  • Dental bridges
  • Inlays and onlays
  • Removable dentures
  • Bruxism management

Cosmetic Dentistry

Cosmetic dentistry is dental work that focuses on improving the appearance of your smile. Kho Dental Clinic offers the following treatments that help enhance the looks of your teeth and your confidence:

  • Braces
  • Clear aligners like Invisalign and Zenyum
  • Dental veneers
  • Professional teeth whitening or take-home teeth whitening

Pediatric Dentistry

Dental care should start from a young age. Pediatric caters to children who have just developed their teeth, to 13 years of age. Some of the key services that Kho Dental Clinic provides to your growing children are:

  • Dental examinations
  • Tooth extractions
  • Dental fillings
  • Orthodontic treatment
  • Root canal treatment
  • Professional dental cleaning
  • Routine dental maintenance
